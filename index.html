Who We Are

Vennitah Beauty Salon is located at Poa Place opposite New Life Medical Clinic, Baraton and was founded in 2018. The salon offers various natural hair care services such as grooming, single twist (coils), 2x twist, rod-set locks, cornrows / braids, 2x twist extensions, afro cut rod- set / straw set and more. They also provide other hair care services like chemical/color services and conditioning services.

Our Mission and Goals

To serve customers with integrity by being loyal to them, the company and ourselves. To give superior customer service consistently with happy relentless attitude while standing within affordable budget for families.
